CHASE TO THE CUT

Presented By

Day 1

Every creation becomes wonderful when it has the ability to entertain and make the audience laugh. We develop a relationship with the moments we capture and the graphics and visuals serve as cherry on the top. In these dull and difficult times when everyone is downhearted and bored, here is your chance to use your imagination and creativity to entertain and bring a smile to gloomy faces. Here is your chance to mix your creativity with the outstanding techniques presented to you by this fast-moving world. The Heritage School invites you to showcase your talent in the field of videography.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

1. Number of Participants : 6+1 (Class 9-12)
2. Duration: 3+1 minutes
3. Acts ranging from mime to ad spoofs will also be allowed
4. Proper usage of graphics & visuals will lead to bonus points
5. The video file size should not exceed 400mb.
6. Exceeding the time limit will lead to points deduction.
