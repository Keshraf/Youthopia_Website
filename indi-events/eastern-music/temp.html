ANTARAAGA

Presented By

Day 2

Music gives soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination and life to everything. It is God’s little reminder that there is something else besides us in this universe – a harmonic connection between all living beings, everywhere, even the stars! Gurudev Rabindranath Tagore had once said, “Music fills the infinite between two souls.” - so we, at The Heritage School, invite all such talented singers and musicians to come forward and fill the infinite with melody and rhythm while showcasing their talents on this platform.

RULES

1. No. of participants: 3-6
2. Duration: 3+2 minutes
3. Participants are to perform a mashup of any Hindustani/Bollywood classical/ semi- classical/ light melody songs
4. Participants are to record their tracks and submit it in the form of a video
5. The performance should be such that each participating member should be heard and seen playing/ singing their parts in the video.
6. Use of autotune is strictly prohibited. If caught, it will lead to immediate disqualification.
7. Songs containing offensive words and inappropriate lyrics will not be allowed.
8. Originality will be given more weightage.

JUDGING CRITERIA

Vocals
Music Production (instruments played)
Synchronization
Clarity
Presentation (Video)
