Day 1

Do you have a unique talent? The Heritage School believes that every student is special and has remarkable talents. Youthopia brings to you Carpe Diem, a talent show where students from various schools will showcase their talents to you. There is nothing more fun or memorable for students than to see their friends celebrated front‐and‐center! Participating in a talent show gives all students recognition for non‐academic talents. So just live in the moment and show us what you’ve got!

RULES

1. Participants per school: 1+1 (Class 9-12)
2. Duration: 2 minutes per school.
3. Every participant will get a minute to showcase their talent.
4. The video must not contain any type of vulgar content. It will lead to disqualification.
