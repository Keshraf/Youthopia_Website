Day 2

“Any kind of dancing is better than no dancing at all.” - Lynda Barr Throughout centuries, dance has been a form of performing art that has enraptured everyone. From Swing Dancing in the 20’s to Moonwalking in the 80’s and a little bit of everything in the 2010’s, dance has evolved substantially. Feel the rhythm of the beat course through your veins, hear the adrenaline pump in your ears and jump onto the stage to show us your moves! Portray your ‘journey through sound’ using movement in a way only YOU can. We, The Heritage School, take pleasure in welcoming all you happy feet to showcase your skills in dancing at BALTER - The Western Dance Event of Youthopia’21.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

1. No of participants - 5+2 (editor included) (Class 10-12)
2. Duration: 5+2 minutes
3. A complete video of the performance needs to be submitted.
4. Participating schools are free to shoot their videos as per their choice. However, 1 meter distance should be maintained between the participants if they choose to shoot it together in a group.
5. Points shall be deducted for exceeding the time limit.
6. Songs containing offensive language should not be used or the word must be faded away
7. Costume changing is allowed

JUDGING CRITERIA

Choreography
Coordination
Costume
Presentation
Expressions

THEME

Journey through sound
