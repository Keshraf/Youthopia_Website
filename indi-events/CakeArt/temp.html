Let your imagination take flight on the empty canvas that a cake is, to delight our sense of taste and sight. The Heritage School invites you to re-explore the art of France in your 8-inch world by translating your own aesthetic in Galerie de Gateau- our Cake Decorating Event.

RULES

1. 1 participant per school ( classes 10-12)
2. Duration: 2 hours to decorate and 30 minutes for editing and submission.

INSTRUCTIONS REGARDING CAKE

Store bought or handmade plain sponge cakes of about 7-8 inches should be used as base. Preview of cake base in the video is required.
A clean coat of any colour should be applied to the cake before decoration. At least two layers of cake should be used.
Use of purchased accessories allowed as long as decoration is not upstaged
Participants are free to use whipped cream, buttercream, royal icing, fondant or ganache. Use of piping bags and nozzles is allowed.

INSTRUCTIONS REGARDING THE VIDEO

The entire process of decoration should be recorded and edited such that the video is at most 5 minutes long (voiceover is optional).
Participants should be wearing a headdress and apron along with the school uniform. The video should be made in a clean set up.
It should include pictures of Plain sponge before icing, picture of the final decorated cake (top and side view), final cake in the set up table (cut in portions for two) and a video of the whole process

INSTRUCTIONS FOR TABLE SET UP

Creative but subtle table layout which compliments the cake
Plain tablecloth
Cutlery and dishes
Napkins and flower Arrangement

JUDGING CRITERIA

Decoration
Presentation
Creativity & Innovation
Basic Skills of layering, piping & decoration
Table layout
