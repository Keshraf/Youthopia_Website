MASQUED

Presented By

Brought to you by

Day 1

MASQUED is a fun event that is all about embodying your artistic creativity on a face. Being able to transform a face into something else is the ultimate form of artistry. MASQUED gives you a platform to use faces as a canvas and transfigure them into something beautiful, doesn’t that sound exciting? Are you ready to splash some colours on a few faces?

THEME

Venetian Masks

RULES

1. Number of participants: 1+1 (1 painter and 1 volunteer if needed). However, if a participant is unable to find a volunteer , then they’re welcome to paint their own faces. (Class 9-12)
2. Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes.

REQUIREMENTS

Keep a basic painting kit with all the items you need. For example : 5 paint brushes and paints according to your choices
Kindly avoid using any harmful chemicals or paints that might be unsuitable for the volunteer’s face.
Participants must be creative and original with their work.
The time limit should not exceed.
The participants must make a time-lapse while painting their face and take pictures at the end.

JUDGING CRITERIA

Uniqueness of idea
Creativity
Skill and technique
Overall presentation
