Day 2

In uncertain times like the contemporary one where a pandemic is at large, we can all use a handsome dose of laughter to lighten and brighten and straighten out our otherwise drab day. Do you think you can tickle the audience’s funny bone and can genuinely make them laugh hard? Well, then, what are you waiting for? Take the risk, explore, and communicate whatever you think, perceive, and to be funny about the world.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

1. Number of participants: 1 ( Classes 11-12)
2. Duration per speaker: 4 minutes.
3. Each participant will have to prepare their videos on a topic of their choice.
4. Use of foul or unparliamentary language is strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.
5. Avoid creating content which might be controversial, and which may hurt the sentiments of the audience.
6. If any comic is found to be copying any comedian’s sketches on the internet it will lead to disqualification.
