ARPEGGIO

Presented By

Day 3

One of the most awaited events in every Youthopia ever, unfortunately online, Arpeggio (Western Music) is the event where the hardcore teenage music enthusiasts come together to work as a band & perform while representing their school and compete for the place at the top.

RULES

1. Maximum number of participants: 7 (Class 9-12)
2. Maximum duration: 5 minutes
3. Participants are to record their song and submit it in the form of a video.
4. Participants can cover a total of 1 song.
5. Participants are free to use any musical instruments for their covers.
6. Video of all participants is mandatory in the final video.
7. Audio effects are allowed to be used apart from autotuning
8. Wearing uniforms is mandatory
9. Only English songs will be permitted.

JUDGING CRITERIA

Production of sound: how does the mix sound and how do the instruments fit together?
Production of the video: how creative is the video and how does it attract the attention of the audience?
Musical creativity: what is changed in the song to make it unique to the participants?
Musicality: the technical ability of musicians on their instruments
