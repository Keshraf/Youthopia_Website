Day 1

Comic Strip

Comics are something that people have always loved reading over the years, whether it be Marvel, DC or a manga from Japan like “Attack On Titan”. They have massive fan bases to the point they can even get adapted into some of the most famous movies or shows on the planet! Comics allow the author to tell their readers a story which leaves a lasting impression on them. We, at Youthopia ‘21, invite you to tell us your story.

RULES

• Number of participants: 1 (Class 10-12)
Each participant will have 4 hours + 30 minutes
• The comic should not be less than 3 pages (no upper page limit)
• The topics will be distributed among the participants 5 minutes before the event starts.
• Any software can be used, but it should be digital. Avoid drawing traditionally.
• The comic may be coloured. It need not be coloured if that matches the theme, but should be shaded well.
• Each page should be divided into at least 3 horizontal panels.
• Proportions must be 1:1, with a resolution of at least 1080×1080.
• May have dialogue. Need not have dialogue if that matches the theme, but then it should depict the theme clearly.
• Participants must send a timelapse video(s) of not more than 5 minutes of them making the comic.
• Submissions will not be accepted after the 30 mins of buffer time.

JUDGING CRITERIA

• Interpretation and relevance to theme
• Creativity and originality
• Quality of art
• Quality of plot
• Arrangement and neatness
