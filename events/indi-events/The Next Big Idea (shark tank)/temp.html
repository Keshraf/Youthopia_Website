Day 2

Shark Tank

“A business has to be involving, it has to be fun, and it has to exercise your creative instincts.” Richard Branson Every crisis gives rise to new ideas. We are facing the pandemic crisis so what is your “new idea”? We invite you to be innovative, bring out your inner entrepreneur, put on your thinking caps, come up with innovative products according to the industries allotted to you but don’t forget all the project details and the pandemic crisis.

RULES

• Number of participants- 2+1
• Duration- 5+3 minutes
• Industries will be allotted 15 hours prior to the event.
• Participants must prepare a PowerPoint presentation which should include the Problem, Solution, SWOT, Marketing Strategy, Sources of Funds, Allocation of funds and Revenue Model, Unique selling proposition. Extra deliverables are appreciated.
• Points shall be deducted for exceeding the time limit.
• Plagiarism won’t be tolerated; it will lead to disqualification.
• All the participants will have to pitch equally during the event
• There will also be a SURPRISE round for the top four teams.

JUDGING CRITERIA

• Presentation
• Content
• Creativity
• Feasibility of the idea
• Answering questions directed by the judges and audience


The First 15 schools to register will be selected.
