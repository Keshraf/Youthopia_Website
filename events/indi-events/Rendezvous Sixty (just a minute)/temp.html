RENDEZVOUS SIXTY

Presented By

Day 2

Just A Minute

Rendezvous Sixty (JAM) is an event that is all about the control of the mind over the mouth. In totality, the competition is an interesting platform for students to engage in public speaking in a game-like situation. This event encourages students to come out of their shells and develop their confidence to communicate in English. Can you make it through sixty seconds of non-stop talking without hesitation, repetition, or deviation? Or will the sheer pressure make you crumble and have your competitors pounce on you in an instant?

Number of participants: 1 (Class 11-12) Total Duration: 1 minute

RULES

• Participants must display originality. Any kind of plagiarism will lead to negative marking.
• The topic will be given 30 seconds prior to the performance.
• The content shouldn’t be offensive or unethical.
• Topic will be given through an online spin-the-wheel.

THE FOLLOWING MAY LEAD TO DEDUCTION IN MARKS

• Speaking too fast or too slow
• Grammatical errors
• Undue stress on words, undue emphasis in the sentences.
• Repetition of previously used ideas

JUDGING CRITERIA

• Composition
• Development of ideas
• Skill, technique, and process
• Depth and breadth of composition
• Overall presentation
• Language, Grammar, Vocabulary
