Day 1

Stock Exchange is an event to simulate trading while learning in stock markets among the participants. The event is designed in such a way that it will not only make participants learn the art of trading but will check their fundamentals regarding capital markets. This event will consist of two rounds.

PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT

The Portfolio Management round will require the participants to work on a specific case study, enabling them to prepare a suitable stock portfolio that ensures a decent return on investments in the long run. The investments are to be made beforehand according to the conditions and circumstances provided and presented during the event. Each team would be provided with a certain time for their pitch and cross-questioning and will be evaluated accordingly. Therefore, the participants must use their financial acumen to put together a portfolio to establish and ensure that the case study's financial goals are achieved.

TRADING ROUND

The participants will be provided with a trading platform, and they are expected to time the market while making short-term trades to ensure profitability in the short term. These purchases of equity common stock should be made based on news provided to them by the organizers. The winners will be decided on their portfolio value and not their liquidity.

RULES AND GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS (FOR PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT)

1. Each team must consist of 2 members only (Class 11-12)
2. Pitch time: 5 minutes
3. Cross-questioning time : 3 minutes
