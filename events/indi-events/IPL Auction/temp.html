IPL Auction

Indian Premier League (IPL) is one of the most awaited annual sports events in India, gluing everyone to their television sets and making them adjust their routines so that they can watch the match without any disturbances. We want to give a platform to students to put their brains to work and form their dream teams. So, here we have “The IPL Auction” where teams of different schoolsfight it out to form the best IPL team without exceeding the budget and keeping all the other rules in mind.

RULES

• No. of participants- 1+1 (Class 11-12)
• Decision of the judges will be final.
• The list of players, their base prices and total budget will be announced one hour prior to the event.
• Each team is required to make a team of minimum 15 players. Failure to do so will result in disqualification.
• Further instructions will be given in the event WhatsApp group.
• Note - First 8 schools to register will be able to participate
