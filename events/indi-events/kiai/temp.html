Day 2

Martial Arts is a tradition of combat practices. It is practiced for a number of reasons, including self-defence, competition, physical health, and entertainment. It is extremely diverse with hundreds of different styles. Consistent training not only conditions the mind and body to have strength and stamina to fight back in a violent situation, but also helps the body fight disease, stay flexible, strong and active as people age. RULES

1. Number of participants: 4 (2 male and 2 female)
2. Two members from each team (one male, one female) will participate in the weapons category. The other two will participate in patterns.
3. Participants should wear their martial arts uniform

PATTERN CATEGORY

Participants have to send a video performing only one pattern.

Judgement category for patterns:-
1. Technical Content.
2. Degree of difficulty, control, agility and accuracy
3. Kicks (proper foot position, height, focus, balance, power, etc.)
4. Hand Techniques (proper hand position, speed, power, tension, etc.)
5. Balance, Confidence, Eye Contact, Intensity

WEAPONS CATEGORY


Participants are allowed to use any 1 of the weapons mentioned below:-
1. Nunchuks
2. Sword
3. Sticks

Judgement category for weapons:
1. Creativity
2. Movement
3. Technique
