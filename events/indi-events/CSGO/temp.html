CSGO

In this first-person shooter, gamers from all over the world can work together to complete objectives or eliminate the enemy. Each player can either join the counter-terrorist team or the terrorist team for that particular round, with each team having its own special features or weapons. Counter Strike is often intense, but this never makes it anything less than amazing fun. It is a never-ending adventure, and one that will improve your gaming skills really quickly without you even realising.

RULES

• No. of participants - 5 playing as a team. (Classes 9-12)
• Each team must present 1 representative. They are responsible for their team & will be responsible for choosing maps. Communication with moderators must be done through the team captain.
• The decisions taken by the moderators will be final.
• Teams not ready within 15 minutes of their match will be disqualified
• In-game chat communication should be limited to questions or requests to the server admin (including technical help), and should not directly target the opponent team or be interpreted as insulting.
• The first team to win 16 rounds in their match will be declared the victor and advance to the next round of the tournament
• The map each team will play will be picked through a map veto. The map pool is Inferno, Train, Mirage, Nuke, Overpass, Dust 2 and Cache.
• The tournament will be played with the following rule set:
- Match: Every series will follow a Best of 1 (Bo1) map format.
- Rounds: 30 Rounds (15 as each side: terrorists & counterterrorists)
- Victory Conditions: The first team to win 16 rounds wins the map.
- Round Duration: Each individual round lasts 1:55 min
- Overtime: In case of a tie after the regulation rounds end (15-15), 6 extra overtime rounds will be played (3 rounds as T & 3 as CT). First team to win 4 rounds is the victor (repeat if necessary). Overtime restarts with $10,000 for both teams.
- Veto: Captains will take turns banning maps to finalise on one.
