PICTURESQUE SPIEL

Presented By

Day 2

The idea of colours floating across a sheet of paper forming either familiar shapes or new creations is appealing to the eye. However, it’s much more than just beads of colour. Photography can captivate someone’s heart and soul. Even the simplest photo has a story. With a story hidden in every nook and corner of the environment around you, The Heritage School gives you a chance to narrate a story and tell us what you see captured in that still.

RULES

1. Number of participants: 2 (Class 11-12)
2. Each participant will be allowed a maximum of THREE entries. Submissions will not be accepted beyond the deadline.
3. Information with regards to the process of submission of entries will be given to the participants in due time.
4. Photos submitted must be in ‘.jpeg’ format. RAW file format is not accepted.
5. Images submitted by participants should be original with EXIF details for authentication. Any image without this information will be liable to be disqualified. Authenticity is MANDATORY.
6. Participants are required to provide a unique title and a short description of your point of view for each image submitted within a maximum 500 characters. All images should be clearly connected to the theme of the event.
7. All submitted photos must not have any border(s), logo(s), copyright marks, watermarks or signatures.
8. Basic editing including cropping, colour saturation of the photo is acceptable, provided any such editing does not affect the authenticity and/or originality of the Photo. No photoshopped pictures will be allowed. Participants with such an entry will be disqualified.
9. Submitted photos must be original and photographed by the contestant. It must also not infringe the copyright, trademark, moral rights, rights of privacy/publicity or intellectual property rights of any person or entity
10. Participants may submit colour or black and white photographs.
