EONS O' VOGUE

Presented By

Day 3

Fashion has been a part of our lives since time immemorial. From Suzy Parker to Gigi Hadid, we have had the opportunity to be inspired and influenced into designing our own sartorial statements. If you know what appeals to you and are beguiled by the attires worn in The 1950s through the 2010s, we urge you to come forward and showcase your artistry and ingenuity. The stage is yours and YOU set the trend. The Heritage School welcomes all you trailblazing individuals and schools to power up under the spotlight and put your designs on display at Eons O’ Vogue – The Fashion Show of Youthopia’21.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

1. Total number of participants: 8+2 (Classes 11-12)
2. Total Duration: 5+1 minutes
3. Each participant from all teams must submit a paragraph of not more than 200 words stating where they got their inspiration for the outfit
4. Please mention all the sources, sites and resources such as Pinterest, Instagram etc. that you use
5. The outfit ideas must be original. The school will be disqualified upon signs of plagiarism or vulgarity. The apparel must follow all norms of decency.

JUDGING CRITERIA

Depiction of theme
Wardrobe and Co-ordination
Presentation
Walk

THEME

Era of fashion from the 1950s to 2010s. You may choose a particular decade or you may depict all the decades consecutively as you see fit. You can represent the theme in any way but it must be understandable to both the audience and the judges.
