BATTLE OF BRAINS

Presented By

Day 3

Chess

Let’s set new calculations of war. Whether an intellectual or a beginner, it is a game for all. The one who wants to evolve, explore and excel mentally and cognitively. So let’s meet on black and white squares and test each other’s IQs. Yes, it is Chess! So, give a chance to your brains to nourish and better keep your opponent away from it. We at The Heritage School, invite all the fighters who are ready to face this war at Youthopia’21.

Number of participants: 1 (Class 11-12) Total Duration: 1 minute

RULES

• Number of participants: 1 (Class 9-12)
• Duration: 10 minutes each in Blitz style, one match.
• The matches will be conducted on www.chess.com. Participants are requested to create an account on the website with their names and a short form of their school’s name (example: NameTHS)
• This is a knockout tournament, each participant will get to play only one match.
• All the participants should accept friend requests of the event heads.
• According to the fixture, one participant will be asked to send a 10 minute match link to the participant as notified on the day of the tournament.
• In case of stalemate and draw, the participants will get to play 3+0 minutes Blitz match again.
• A resignation or match abandonment by any one will be considered a fair win for the other.
• Judges’ decision will be final.
