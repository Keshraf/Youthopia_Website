ON AIR

Presented By

Day 3

If you are a chatterbox at heart and brimming with wit - unleash your imagination on this immortal platform of Youthopia. Spin your magic! Mesmerize the audience... Now is the time for you to go ‘ON AIR’.

THEME

'Resonance from Yesteryears' - A trip down the memory lane to cherish the past.

RULES

1. The recommended performance time is 3 minutes, with the maximum being 4 minutes.
2. One song to be played for not more than 30 seconds in the middle of the performance (to be counted within the set time limit) and end with a song not more than 30 seconds.
3. The second song will not be counted within the set time limit.
4. Participants will have to send the edited audio files in mp3 format.
5. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
6. The participants are not allowed to take their or their school's name during their performance. All the names should be fictitious as maintaining anonymity is imperative.

JUDGEMENT CRITERA

Content (incorporation of the theme)
Presentation (voice tonality, modulation, pitching, connect with the listeners)
Overall creativity (usage of time and words, scripting, innovative ways of presentation, audio editing, choice of songs)
