IMPASSE

Presented By

Brought to you by

Day 1 & 2

Difficulties are often entailed by all businesses from different sectors. What seems applauding in real life scenarios is the way these businesses tackle those circumstances. Thus, Youthopia presents to you “Impasse”. Put your critical thinking skills to the test to get results. You are expected to deal with the crisis your company faces and prevent this catastrophic disaster from engulfing your company name. Act as a peacemaker for survival. Combine strategies to combat disruptive and unforeseen conflicts. Overturn the unfavorable situations and save your public image for you will have to break through the “impasse”.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

1. Number of Participants: 2 members in each team (Class 10-12)
2. The first 25 schools to register will be selected.

ROUND 1: Corporate Crisis

Preparation Time: 60 minutes, Presentation Time: 4+2 minutes
Each team will be given a hypothetical corporate crisis 60 minutes prior to the commencement of this round. Thus, the teams will have to come up with a presentation and a pitch explaining the ways in which they plan to tackle the crisis given to them. A rebuttal round will follow the pitch.
Only 10 teams will be moving forward to the final round.

ROUND 2: Financial Crisis

Preparation Time: 45 minutes, Pitching Time: 5+2 minutes
The teams will be given a hypothetical financial crisis 45 minutes prior to the event and they will have to come up with a presentation and a pitch explaining the strategies that they plan to employ in order to overcome the financial crisis and win back the trust of their shareholders. Following each pitch, there will be a rebuttal round. Qualifying schools will have the liberty of asking questions to each other in this round. The final winners of the event shall be declared following this.

JUDGING CRITERIA

Presentation
Knowledge
Q/A
Confidence
Rebuttal round
