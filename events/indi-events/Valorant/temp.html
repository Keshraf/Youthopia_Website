Valorant

VALORANT is a 5v5 character-based tactical FPS where precise gunplay meets unique agent abilities. The Heritage School invites talented gamers and leaders to its first ever Valorant Event as many teams go head to head against each other in an action packed quest for glory and compete for the title.

RULES

• Number of participants - 5 on a team for main roster and 1 or 2 substitutes. (Class 9-12)
• Each team must have a captain/representative responsible for the team and for vetoing maps.
• The matches will be carried out in the following way: Every series will be a Best of 1 (Bo1) excluding finals which is to be decided later according to time allotment.
• Possible number of rounds: 24, 12 on each side (defenders/attackers)
• Victory Conditions: First team to win 13 rounds will take the series. Each round lasts 100 seconds with 30 seconds of warmup. Starting side will be chosen with a side draw/knife round.
• Overtime: In case of an overtime (12-12 scoreline), as per the in-game overtime rules, sides will be swapped and an overtime of 2 rounds will begin, which can be looped until a definite winner is declared.
• Overtime starts with 5000 credits.
• All Maps in the game will be available during map draw.
• Map Pool: Split, Haven, Icebox, Bind, Ascent, Breeze
• All participants must be in Discord voice channels.
• Cheating/exploiting will not be tolerated and decorum should be maintained.
