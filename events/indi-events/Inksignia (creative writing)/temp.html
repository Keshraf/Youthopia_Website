Day 1

Creative Writing

Creative writing has always been a great medium of self-expression, channelling creativity and imagination into a connected composition. In our current scenario, it is imperative that we express ourselves as best we can and in what way could be better than using our eloquence and vocabulary. Our creative writing competition, Inksignia, gives every participant the platform to compose and present their pieces, in their own signature styles. This opportunity allows participants to express themselves and make productive use of their creativity from the comfort of their homes, and be part of this wonderful, rewarding experience.

Number of participants: 1 (Class 11-12) Total Duration: 1 minute

RULES

• Number of participants: 1 per language (Hindi/English/Bengali) (Class 9-12)
• Duration: 60 minutes; topics will be given 30 minutes prior to the event.
• Word limit: 500-650 words.
• Participants will be supervised on Google meet while they are writing their essay.
• At the end of the time limit, the essay is to be scanned and submitted as a single PDF, within 10 minutes.
• Handwriting must be decent and legible.

JUDGING CRITERIA

• Composition
• Content
• Vocabulary
• Structure and continuity
• Grammar and punctuation
• Creativity
