RULES

1. Duration: 3hr 30mins. Each round will be of 1.5 hours (30mins buffer time)
2. Number of participants: 2 in each team (Class 10-12)
3. ROUND 1: Each team will be given a wallet of 15cr 10lacs. The given amount must be used wisely to buy majority shares of 2 companies in the auction as a whole. There will be 2 tiers, each consisting of twelve companies. Each team must acquire one company from both the tiers. Throughout the auction, the wallet must always have at least 10lakhs in it.
4. ROUND 2: Presentation time for each team: 5+2 mins. After completion of the auction, the teams will have to form a joint venture between the two companies that they bought in the auction and give a presentation on the new product or service that will be launched by their joint venture to convince the judges that it would be a hot sell.
5. Each endeavour will be pitched to the judges along with a presentation and a question/answer round. (list of companies will be provided later)
6. The first 10 schools to register would be considered for participation.
7. The presentations must include all basic features that the two companies need to consider while forming a joint venture.
