MISSION IMPROBABLE

Presented By

Day 2

Treasure Hunt

The Hidden Quest Treasure Hunt takes participants on a cryptic, interactive journey into the secret nooks and crannies of a location of your choice. In this pandemic situation, we present you Mission Improbable. Each team needs collective brainpower in order to find a piece of information. Fields of science, literature, geography, history and many more would be applied including the use of references from TV shows and movies... The use of different media platforms would be taken into consideration as well. It will help you to develop new skills , strengthen and reinforce other skills such as leadership, communication, and problem solving.

RULES

• Number of participants: 2-4 (Classes 10-12)
• Duration: 1 hour
• First Team to solve the last clue would be declared the winner.
• Using Google would be allowed.
• Communication between participants will happen via Discord rooms.
• The event would start at the given time, participants joining late would lose time.
• Pen And paper would be required to solve the clues.
• Password protected documents can only be opened by the answer to the previous one.
• You will get only 2 clues in total if you are stuck.
